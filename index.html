Why fake news?

The rise of fake news highlights the erosion of long-standing institutional bulwarks against misinformation in the internet age. Concern over the problem is global. However, much remains unknown regarding the vulnerabilities of individuals, institutions, and society to manipulations by malicious actors.

How ICT Helps Combat Fake News

Fake news is widespread in today's society, leading to mistrust in online news sources. Many social media users share news without verifying its credibility, assuming that online content is automatically trustworthy.

Truthify aims to provide users with tools and awareness to combat misinformation. While the platform is limited to online users, its influence can extend far beyond traditional methods, making information dissemination easier and faster.

Key Concepts in Fake News

Mistrust

Fake news aims to make you doubt your existing beliefs and knowledge.

Misinformation

Fake news misleads, spreading false knowledge with malicious or humorous intent.

Manipulation

Fake news persuades and influences people to align with particular opinions.
